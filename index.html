Concept&Mint

"SaDior" in Mandarin means "adorable goofball." The "SaDiorMETA" is the world's first NFTrash, which stands for Digi-Junk, a new category of NFTs. The Sadior Metaverse tells the story of a Digi-Junk prophet named Mr. Gdog from the year 2413, hailing from the original SaDiorMETA. He time-travels to 2024 to deliver a message to today's NFT enthusiasts who are struggling with the allure of digital collectibles: "Do not trust the enchantment of digital treasures; instead, join the ranks of NFTrash." Each Sadior serves as the sole token to enter this comical universe, to be cherished as an heirloom and passed down through generations.
